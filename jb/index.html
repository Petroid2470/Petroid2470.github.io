Kindle 5.6.5 Jailbreak

Instructions

The steps MUST occur in the following order.

  1. Press Stage1.
  2. Press back after page fails to load.
  3. Press Stage2.
  4. Read titlebar for next steps.
  5. Once the title changes to "Jailbreak succeeded!", you're done.

If the Kindle does not open the home screen and the titlebar does not change to "JB starting..." after pressing Stage2, STOP! Do not continue attempting to execute the jailbreak. It is incompatible with your system.
